Stein  (England)   05-2024

We had a fabulous stay in Almuñécar. The pictures are exactly what it looks like - gorgeous view. We had a family of 6 - four adults and two children. The family took the two bedrooms on the second floor with en-suite bath, and the grandparents took the bedroom on the top floor with bath next door. We ate on the balcony for dinner and breakfast and enjoyed the lovely view. There are two pools - note that one is closed on Mon and the other closed on Tues. The infinity pool is unbelievable. It is a bit of a walk down some steps (and back up afterwards) but worth it. Pools close at 9. It is about a 10 min drive down a steep curvy path to get to anything, but there are good grocery stores at the bottom. We drove to Alhambra, Nerja, Malaga, etc all from Almuñécar. It was a bit of time in the car, but very doable. Cristobal is a wonderful host. He helped us print some tickets, locate some packing tape, and contacted me when he found my son’s iPod left behind. I don’t typically ask much of my hosts, but he was super helpful!
